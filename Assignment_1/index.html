Fluffy Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk (any kind you like)
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter (plus extra for cooking)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional but sooo good)

Instructions

  1. Mix dry stuff: In a big bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. Mix wet stuff: In another bowl, beat the egg with milk, melted butter, and vanilla extract.
  3. Combine: Pour the wet mix into the dry mix and stir gently until just combined. Donâ€™t overmix â€” itâ€™s okay if itâ€™s a bit lumpy!
  4. Heat the pan: Warm a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. Add a little butter to coat.
  5. Cook pancakes: Pour about 1/4 cup batter per pancake onto the pan. Cook until bubbles appear on the surface and edges look set, about 2-3 minutes. Flip and cook another 1-2 minutes until golden brown.
  6. Serve it up: Stack â€™em high, top with syrup, fresh fruits, chocolate chips, or whatever vibes you want!

Basic Overnight Oats

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup milk (dairy or plant-based)
  • 1/4 cup yogurt (optional, for creaminess)
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon honey or maple syrup (or to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a jar or bowl, mix oats, milk, yogurt, chia seeds, sweetener, and vanilla.
  2. Stir well, cover with a lid or plastic wrap.
  3. Refrigerate overnight (or at least 4 hours).
  4. Next morning, stir, top, and enjoy!
